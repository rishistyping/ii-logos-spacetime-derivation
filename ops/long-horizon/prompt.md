# v0.5 Long-Horizon Prompt

This operational packet coordinates the v0.5 bridge-decomposition build for
`ii-logos-spacetime-derivation`.

## Mission

Promote only narrow algebraic and arithmetic bridge claims that are backed by
local Lean declarations or deterministic computed artifacts.

## Non-goals

- Do not promote the physical light-cone reading.
- Do not promote horizon, redshift, arrow-of-time, or shared-sign claims.
- Do not add an app, chatbot, GitHub Pages site, or Harmonic dependency.
- Do not replace the lightweight `Mat2` / `Vec2` Lean style.

## Authority Order

1. Lean declarations that build with `lake build`.
2. Deterministic SymPy artifacts regenerated by `bash scripts/check_python.sh`.
3. Public truth surfaces in `docs/`, `spec/`, and `README.md`.
4. Interpretive paper narrative.

When these disagree, update the lower-authority surface to match the higher one.

## Boundary Rule

Any statement involving physical light rays, light cones, horizons, redshift,
arrow of time, shared sign, `t > 0`, `c > 0`, or `PHYS-01` must not be tagged
`Lean-proved`.
